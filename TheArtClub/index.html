About

"The Art Club" is an ecommerce website that aims to provide its consumers top-tier service by presenting tools for sale that is used for arts and crafts. Presenting a website that helps artist find particular item they need to complete their own artistic creation. The project features a more accessible way of shopping online for your own art needs in the comfort of your own home. Giving variety and authenticity to service and diversifying your options by presenting a space that lets the consumers browse freely through the site. The project mainly accommodates artists that want to explore art in different mediums thus providing variety in products to offer.
